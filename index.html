Find, Share & Cite Research Data

This repository is built on FAIRspace, a research data management platform designed around the FAIR principles i.e. Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. FAIRspace gives research teams a structured way to organise experiments, samples, and metadata alongside the underlying data files, so nothing gets lost between the lab and long-term storage. Datasets registered here carry rich, standardised metadata and persistent identifiers, making them easy to discover, cite, and reuse, even by people outside the team that produced them. Sensitive and access-controlled data can coexist with openly published datasets, governed by fine-grained permissions defined for each project.

About this repository

This repository is built on FAIRspace, a research data management platform designed around the FAIR principles i.e. Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. FAIRspace gives research teams a structured way to organise experiments, samples, and metadata alongside the underlying data files, so nothing gets lost between the lab and long-term storage. Datasets registered here carry rich, standardised metadata and persistent identifiers, making them easy to discover, cite, and reuse, even by people outside the team that produced them. Sensitive and access-controlled data can coexist with openly published datasets, governed by fine-grained permissions defined for each project.
